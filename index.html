The limitations to what a website could be is an
incredibly thought-provoking point that laurel
brings up in her article. We, as the creators of our
own websites, maintain absolute control and
are able to meticulously fine-tune even the most
insignificant aspects. In turn, our website becomes
a reflection of what we want other viewers to
perceive us as, we are essentially crafting our 'public'
online presence in the form of a website.
